About WBIntel

WBIntel

West Bengal's Real Time Intelligence Dashboard

Everything. Everywhere. All at Once.

WBIntel is a real-time open-source intelligence monitor that aggregates everything happening across all 23 districts of West Bengal — live news, politics, crime, business, weather, social media, and more — into a single dense dashboard.

Built For

📰 Journalists — Monitor breaking stories across all beats and districts in real time

🔬 Political & Policy Analysts — Track political developments, government actions, and civic movements

📺 News Enthusiasts — Stay informed about everything happening in West Bengal, all in one place

🎓 Mass Communication Students — Study real-time news flow, media coverage patterns, and regional journalism

📊 Researchers — Aggregate and analyze information flow across categories and districts

How It Works

All data is fetched live from 20+ public RSS feeds, WeatherAPI.com, X embeds, and YouTube Live. Zero backend — everything runs in your browser. No fake data, no placeholders. If a source fails, you see an error with a retry button.
